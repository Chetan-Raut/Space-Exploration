A Brief Overview of the Sun

The sun is a star located at the center of the solar system. It is a massive ball of hot gas that emits light and heat, which sustain life on Earth. The sun's diameter is about 109 times that of Earth, and it has a surface temperature of about 5,500 degrees Celsius.
